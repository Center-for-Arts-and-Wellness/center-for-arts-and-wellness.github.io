Catwalk/Dance Workshop on Sunday, October 01
Jesse Randhawa is a successful model who has appeared in the Femina Miss India 1994 contest with Aishwarya Rai and Sushmita Sen. She has also guest-starred on the Dance India Dance Grand Finale with husband and choreographer Sandip Soparrkar.
Sandip Soparrkar is an Indian, Latin and Ballroom dancer, Bollywood choreographer and columnist. Soparrkar is the pioneer of 'India Dance Week' and has been honored by the Government of India for his contribution in the field of dance, choreography and social work. Soparrkar is a Dance teacher to celebrities like Beyonce, Shakira, Hrithik Roshan, Priyanka Chopra and more!
