RAGA-RHYTHYM: 2020 MUSIC and DANCE Competition (Virtual)

We are excited to announce our RAGA~RHYTHM Competitions for Indian Musicians and Dancers from all across the United States!

Performance is a critical part of arts training, and we are delighted to offer a platform for talent to be showcased, celebrated, and recognized by audiences across the world. Entries will be evaluated by a panel of judges on the basis of technique, creativity, performance and more.

HALLOWEEN SCAVENGER HUNT (Social Distancing - Virtual Event)

The Center for Arts and Wellness invites families and friends, children and adults, to our first annual Halloween Scavenger Hunt event! The event is open to participants from all across the United States!

Participants will drive around their neighborhoods to find Halloween themed scavenger hunt items, each with points. Points can be also earned by dressing up and for most creative team name!

Participants will be given a list of scavenger hunt items at the start of the event. Teams will have 1.5 hours to find and take pictures of the objects and upload on our Facebook Event page. At the end of the event, the team with maximum points will win prizes.

Date and Time: Saturday, October 31, 2020; 3.30–5:00 pm EST, 2:30-4:00 pm CST, 12:30-2:00 pm PST, etc. (All participants irrespective of the location will start the game at the same time.)
Register your team by clicking Here.
Entry Fees - $15/ team
Registration & Fee Payment: by October 30, 2020

VERVE 2020 - Festival of Performing Arts

The Center for Arts and Wellness is pleased to bring to you our 2nd Annual Festival of Performing Arts - VERVE 2020 on Sunday, May 17.

Join us for an extravaganza of artistically choreographed dances and creatively composed songs and renditions of classical music by young and adult students from the New England area.

The MUSIC Section will be held in the morning-afternoon time frame and will feature Indian (Hindustani and Carnatic styles, vocal and instrumental) and Western Music (Chorus, Piano, Guitar ensembles).

The DANCE Section will be held in the afternoon-evening time frame and will feature Indian dances such as Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Kuchipudi, Odissi, Folk, and Bollywood and International dances such as Chinese, Ballet, Tap, Lyrical, Modern, Jazz, etc.

Location: Trottier Middle School in Southborough, MA.
*Exact Festival times will be announced in April.
